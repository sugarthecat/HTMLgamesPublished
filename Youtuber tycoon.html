Youtuber Tycoon

Commands:

1. Exit. Type 'e' to exit the game

2. Work. Type 'work' to work for money. If you work enough times, you might earn a higher salary.

3. Ads. Type 'a' to advertise your channel.

4. Upgrade. type 'u' into the console to upgrade your setup, so your videos get more views.

5. Gaming Video. type 'g' into the console to make a gaming video. Then Type into the console what it says to type

6. Vlog. Type 'v' to make a vlog. Then type into the console what it says to type.

7. Skit. Type 's' to make a skit video. Then type into the console what it says to type.

8. Music Video. Type 'm' to make a music video. Then type into the console what it says to type.

9. Data. type 'd' to see your channel's analytics.

10. Save. type 'save' to save your progress.

11. Load. Type 'load' to load your progress

Enjoy!

A small note. even if you save, it compresses data, rounding down, leaving you with a toll on your subs.
